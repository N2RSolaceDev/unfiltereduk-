Privacy Without Compromise

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At unfiltereduk.co.uk, we believe in digital freedom, privacy, and resistance to surveillance. We do not require government ID, phone numbers, or biometric data to use our service. Your right to anonymous expression is protected by design.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information — transparently and minimally.

2. Information We Collect

Public Domain (@unfiltereduk.co.uk)

  • Email Address: Must be in the format you@unfiltereduk.co.uk. No invite required.
  • Full Name & Avatar: For identification within the network.
  • Messages: Encrypted in transit, stored securely. You own your content.

Custom Domains (Invite-Only)

Access to custom domains (e.g., @yourorg.co.uk) is by invitation only and granted to trusted partners. No user ID is ever required.

Technical Data

  • IP Addresses: Logged for 90 days to prevent abuse, spam, and attacks.
  • Login Timestamps: To detect unauthorized access.

We never share IPs or logs with third parties for advertising or tracking.

3. When (And If) We Ask for ID

We never ask for ID during registration, login, or normal use.

However, if automated systems detect:

  • Patterns of illegal activity (e.g., threats, CSAM, large-scale fraud)
  • Severe abuse (botting, phishing, impersonation)

Then — and only then — may we:

  • Temporarily flag the account
  • Optionally request identity info to assess legitimacy
  • Use it solely to determine if harm was intended

This request is not mandatory, but may affect whether access is restored.

We reject pre-crime surveillance. You are not guilty until proven so — and we will not become a tool of digital authoritarianism.

4. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • TLS encryption in transit
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords
  • Secure MongoDB with access controls
  • Encrypted backups (deleted after 30 days)

No system is 100% secure — use responsibly.

5. Disclosure of Data — Only in Extreme Cases

We will not hand over user data for political, ideological, or minor legal reasons.

We may disclose information only if:

  • There is credible evidence of imminent harm (e.g., threats of violence, terrorism)
  • A binding court order is issued in a life-threatening investigation
  • Required to prevent death or serious injury

Even then:

  • Disclosure is narrowly scoped
  • We will notify you unless legally prohibited
  • We will publicly report such requests when allowed
  • We will challenge overreach in court

6. Your Rights

You always have the right to:

  • Access all your data
  • Export your messages and profile
  • Delete your account permanently
  • Correct your information
