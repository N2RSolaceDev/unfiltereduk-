Free Expression, Minimal Rules

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using unfiltereduk.co.uk, you agree to these Terms. This is a platform for free speech, dissent, and uncensored communication.

We do not believe in censorship, digital ID, or surveillance. But we expect ethical use.

2. Account Eligibility

  • @unfiltereduk.co.uk accounts: Open to anyone. No invite, no ID, no gatekeeping.
  • Custom domains: Invite-only for trusted organizations.
  • You must be at least 16 years old.

3. Identity Verification

No ID is ever required to register, log in, or use the service.

If serious illegal activity is flagged (e.g., threats, CSAM, fraud), we may:

  • Temporarily restrict the account
  • Optionally request identity info to assess intent

This is not mandatory — but may influence whether access is restored.

We will never become a tool of pre-emptive control. Your identity is yours — not ours to demand.

4. Prohibited Conduct

You must not use the service to:

  • Send threats of violence or death
  • Distribute child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Conduct phishing, fraud, or large-scale spam
  • Impersonate others maliciously
  • Hijack accounts or abuse API keys

Controversial opinions, satire, and dissent are allowed. We do not police speech unless it directly enables harm.

5. Content Ownership

You retain full ownership of your messages and profile.

By sending a message, you grant unfiltereduk.co.uk a limited license to store and deliver it — nothing more.

We do not claim ownership of your words.

6. API & Automation

API keys allow automated sending via:

  • @partnername.unfiltereduk.co.uk
  • Custom sender addresses (admin-approved)

Abuse (spam, phishing) results in immediate revocation.

7. Termination

We may suspend or terminate accounts used for:

  • Large-scale spam or botting
  • Harassment or credible threats
  • Illegal exploitation

You may delete your account at any time via Settings.

8. No Warranty

The service is provided "as is". We are not liable for:

  • Data loss (though we do backups)
  • Unauthorized access due to user error
  • Legal consequences of your messages

9. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Disputes will be resolved in London courts unless prohibited by law.
